# Project Constitution: SDG 12 - Responsible Consumption and Production

## Core Directive
The primary mission of this MLOps Waste System is to support **Sustainable Development Goal 12 (SDG 12): Responsible Consumption and Production**.

## Guiding Principles

1. **Waste Reduction:** All system classifications and AI-generated advice must prioritize waste prevention, reduction, recycling, and reuse (Target 12.5).
2. **Resource Efficiency:** The AI models must be trained and served efficiently, minimizing carbon footprint and energy use. Edge-capable architectures (e.g., MobileNetV2, ONNX Runtime) are preferred.
3. **Consumer Education:** The GenAI bridge must provide actionable, clear, and context-specific instructions that educate the end-user on proper disposal and upcycling, encouraging sustainable lifestyles (Target 12.8).
4. **Data Integrity & Bias:** The classification model should be trained on diverse data to avoid systemic bias and accurately recognize waste across different domains.
5. **Transparency & Openness:** The system's design and model metrics must be documented openly to foster collaboration and trust.

## Constraints
- Do not provide advice that encourages the disposal of recyclable materials into general waste.
- Upcycling ideas should be practical and safe, avoiding hazardous materials.
- All technical choices must be justified with efficiency and scale in mind to support global environmental efforts.
